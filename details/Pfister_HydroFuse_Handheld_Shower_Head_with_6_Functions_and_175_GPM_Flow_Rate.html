Pfister HydroFuse Handheld Shower Head

Elevate your daily shower routine with this advanced handheld shower head, designed for both comfort and efficiency.

Experience Customized Comfort

With six distinct spray modes — Full Spray, Massage, Full Spray & Massage Combo, HydroFuse, Full Spray & HydroFuse Combo, and Pause — this shower head adapts to your every need. Whether you're looking for a relaxing massage or a refreshing full spray, the intuitive settings make it simple to switch between options and enjoy a truly personalized shower experience.

Smart Water Flow Technology

The innovative HydroFuse technology delivers a bold, yet water-efficient flow. This unique water pattern uses less water while maintaining a stronger feel, thanks to its larger drop design. This handheld shower head features a sleek Spot Defense Matte Black finish that looks stunning in any modern bathroom. Its design not only adds a touch of sophistication but also resists water spots and mineral buildup, ensuring long-lasting beauty without the need for frequent cleaning. The nozzles are easy to maintain with just a quick wipe of a soft damp cloth.

Trusted by Homeowners

Backed by Pfister's Limited Lifetime Warranty, this shower head is built to last. Its high-quality construction ensures reliable performance and comfort year after year, making it a solid investment for your home.
