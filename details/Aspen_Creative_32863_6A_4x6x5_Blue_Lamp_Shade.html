Stylish Washing Blue Denim Lamp Shade

Elevate your home decor with this exquisite washing blue denim lamp shade. Crafted from premium denim fabric, this shade is not only visually striking but also exceptionally durable, ensuring it remains a cherished addition to your space for years to come.

Perfect for Any Room

This 4" top x 6" bottom x 5" slant lamp shade is designed for versatility. Whether you're lighting up a living room, dining area, or bedroom, its Empire hardback shape offers a classic look that's both functional and stylish.
